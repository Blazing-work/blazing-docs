Blazing provides local emulators so developers can iterate without touching production infrastructure.

## What It Provides

- Local substitutes for common services (volumes, secrets, queues, etc.).
- Fast iteration with minimal setup.
- Safe testing without cloud costs.

## Why It Matters

- Faster feedback loops.
- Fewer production dependencies during development.
- Safer testing of workflows and services.

## How To Use

Use the `LocalStack` APIs in development. Your platform team can provide recommended local setups.
