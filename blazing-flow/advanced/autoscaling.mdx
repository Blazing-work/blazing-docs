Blazing Flow uses adaptive autoscaling to keep latency low while controlling cost.

## What It Does

- Scales worker capacity up when demand rises.
- Scales down when the system is idle.
- Applies backpressure when downstream services are saturated.

## Why It Matters

- Faster response times during spikes.
- Lower cost during quiet periods.
- More predictable performance for end users.

## What You Control

Autoscaling policies are managed by **Blazing Core**. Application developers do not need to tune scaling directly.
