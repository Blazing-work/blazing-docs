Blazing Flow automatically optimizes large payloads (DataFrames, arrays, and columnar data) during execution. You do not need to configure anything to benefit from this.

## What You Should Do

- Keep payloads serializable (pandas, NumPy, PyArrow, or basic Python types).
- Prefer references (IDs/paths) over massive inline payloads when possible.
- Use volumes or bucket mounts for large artifacts.

## Best Practices

- For large datasets, write intermediate results to a volume or bucket and pass references between steps.
- Keep step results reasonably sized to avoid network overhead.
