Blazing Gateway is the managed entry point that routes traffic to Blazing workloads.

## What It Does

- Routes requests to the correct region or service
- Supports gradual rollouts and traffic shaping
- Enforces organization‑level policies

Gateway configuration is managed by your platform team.
