Blazing handles all payment processing through Stripe. Invoices are generated automatically at the end of each billing period based on your metered usage.

## Invoices

### Viewing Invoices

Go to **Settings > Organization > Billing** and scroll to the **Invoice History** section. Each invoice shows:

- Invoice number
- Billing period (start and end dates)
- Total amount
- Payment status (Paid, Payment Failed, Past Due)
- Download link for the invoice PDF (hosted by Stripe)

### Invoice Contents

Each invoice includes a detailed breakdown of:

- CPU core-hours by SKU class (spot, on-demand, confidential)
- Memory GiB-hours by SKU class
- Storage GiB-hours by storage class
- Network egress by tier (premium, standard)
- Base plan fee (Team plan: $250/month)
- Total amount due

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings > Organization > Billing**
2. Click **Manage Payment Method** (opens Stripe Billing Portal)
3. Add or update your credit card

Alternatively, during initial setup, you will be prompted to add a payment method through Stripe Checkout. This collects your card without an immediate charge.

### Supported Methods

Blazing accepts all payment methods supported by Stripe, including:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- Bank transfers (where available)

### Billing Portal

The **Manage Billing** button opens Stripe's hosted billing portal where you can:
- Update your payment method
- View all invoices
- Download receipts
- Manage your subscription

## Payment Failures

If a payment fails, Blazing handles it automatically:

1. **Email notification** sent immediately with failure details and next retry date
2. **Smart retries** managed by Stripe (typically retries at 1, 3, 5, and 7 days)
3. **Payment Overdue banner** appears on the billing dashboard
4. **Failure tracking** records the number of consecutive failures

Your workloads continue running during the retry period. Contact support if you need to resolve a payment issue before the automatic retry.

## Billing State

Your organization's billing status is tracked as one of:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | Normal billing state, no issues |
| **Paid** | Most recent invoice paid successfully |
| **Payment Failed** | Payment attempt failed, retry scheduled |
| **Past Due** | Multiple payment retries failed |

The current billing state is visible on the billing dashboard.
